Starbucks

Address: Neumanns gate 25, 5015 Bergen

Starbucks is a very popular place for studying. This Starbucks is small and very cozy. It has 2 areas where you can sit down. Inside and outside. Since it is quite a small place, it is best to work with Individual assignments or read, since it is pretty quiet there.

If you need to use your computer, it is best to sit inside where there is a place for your computer to be charged. There are a lot of other people working on their assignments as well, which will only motivate you.

The outside area is best for reading books or writing notes. In this outside area, the tables are bigger. When it is raining, you can hear the drops hitting against the glass roof. The whole outside area is in glass, so you are able to watch other people go about their day.

Espresso House

Address: Torgallmenningen 9, 5014 Bergen

In the heart of Bergen, Norway lies the Espresso House perfect for writing essays and other assignments. The brown, black and green colors used in the interior design contributes to creating a relaxed and comfortable enviroment. Whether you prefer sitting alone or with your group partners, whether you prefer working with your laptop on your lap or on a table, this Espresso House has several options to meet whatever preference you may have.

Huge round tables with loads of space for your PC and books, smaller tables perfect to fit your laptop and comfortable sofas perfect for group work is a huge advantage of this location, however the most crucial thing has not yet been mentioned: they have loads and loads of sockets, so you will never have to go home to recharge! You will always find students working on something when visiting this Espresso House, so you will never feel out of place or feel like you don’t fit in, because this place is perfect for students like you.

If you have a strong wish to do your studying here you may do that as well, people are after all different and some might not mind noisy enviroments when reading. We do however recommend doing this in the earlier parts of the day when there are not as many people present.

This coffee place’s trendy music, calm ambient noise, comfortable seats and yummy coffee will allow you to fully emerge yourself in your work without the distractions of your gaming pc or consoles getting in your way!
