Espresso House

Address: Torgallmenningen 9, 5014 Bergen

In the heart of Bergen, Norway lies the Espresso House perfect for group projects and other assignments. The brown, black and green colors used in the interior design contributes to creating a relaxed and comfortable enviroment. Whether you prefer sitting alone or with your group partners, whether you prefer working with your laptop on your lap or on a table, this Espresso House has several options to meet whatever preference you may have.

Huge round tables with loads of space for your PC and books and also smaller tables with two chairs make this the ideal coffee place for group work. They also have comfortable sofas perfect for the ones wishing to work more laid back. They also have loads and loads of sockets, so you will never have to go home to recharge!

You will always find students working on something when visiting this Espresso House, so you will never feel out of place or feel like you don’t fit in, because this place is perfect for students like you.

If you have a strong wish to do your studying here you may do that as well, people are after all different and some might not mind noisy enviroments when reading. We do however recommend doing this in the earlier parts of the day when there are not as many people present to interupt you.

This coffee place’s trendy music, calm ambient noise, comfortable seats and yummy coffee will allow you to fully emerge yourself in your work without the distractions of your gaming pc or consoles getting in your way!

Starbucks

Address: Vetrlidsallmenningen 2, 5014 Bergen

This is the first Starbucks that opened in Bergen. It is right above a popular restaurant and brings in many people. It is almost always crowded. This Starbucks is therefore best for group assignments.

There are many big tables, where even a group of 8 or more people can sit down and talk. The awesome smell of coffee is an additional benefit.

Starbucks is the third place for people after home and office. You and your group can sit there in hours, even if all the coffee is over. The background noise will only make you and your group more confident to talk to each other.
